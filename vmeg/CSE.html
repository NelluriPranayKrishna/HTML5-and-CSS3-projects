Computer Science and Engineering(Accredited by NBA)


The field of Computer Science and Engineering has been pushing the envelope of almost all other disciplines, which use computing as a fundamental means of inquiry and tool for discovery. Computer science and engineering offers exciting, intellectually challenging, and rapidly growing career opportunities. It is the heart of where the action is: whether this means intelligent game playing, mobile applications, smart robots, cloud computing, data security, social networks, or bioinformatics.

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in 1999 with an intake of 60 and enhanced to 120 in 2006, 180 in 2012 and 240 in 2013. The department is headed by Dr. N. Samba Shiva Rao, a senior Professor with vast industrial and teaching experience spanning nearly fifteen years. The department comprises 46 well qualified faculty of which 5 are Professors, 11 are Associate Professors, and 30 Assistant Professors.

This program has been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) till 2018.

Computer Science and Engineering has two primary foci: Computer Hardware Design: including an understanding of design methodologies for electronic circuits, digital systems, computer architecture and integrated circuits, and Computer Software Design: including an understanding of designing a software, program, and coding for developing user friendly applications, both online and off line for the use of individuals and corporate clients across the world.
